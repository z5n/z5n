The Long Walk

The Long Walk is a dystopian horror novel by Stephen Kingabout a totalitarian America where 100 teenage boys must walk at a steady pace of four miles per hour without stopping. The last one standing wins "The Prize". The rules are brutal: three warnings for slowing down, and you're shot and killed. It's a grim, psychological story about survival, society, and the nature of entertainment.
